FAQ

How long does service take ?

Our Service time is dependent on both the model and the service required. Of course we can consult for all service needs but we will also do the work that is only needed.

Does the size of my vehicle matter ?

While we mainly deal with personal or small commercial vehicles, we can still do inspections to offer advice on issues.

What if I have custom parts ?

Custom parts and builds can be worked on but might cost more money. Warrenty will also vary depending on mods.
